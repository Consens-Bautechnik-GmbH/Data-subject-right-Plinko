Data Subject Rights

As a user of the Plinko 2.0: Drop & Win mobile game, you have several rights regarding how your personal information is processed. These rights ensure transparency, control, and protection of your data. You may exercise the following rights at any time:

Right of Access – You can request confirmation of whether your personal data is being processed and obtain a copy of that data.
Right to Rectification – You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
Right to Erasure – You may ask for your data to be deleted when it is no longer necessary or when you withdraw consent.
Right to Restrict Processing – You can request limitation of how your data is used under certain conditions.
Right to Data Portability – You may obtain your data in a structured, commonly used format and request its transfer to another controller.
Right to Object – You can object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
Right to Withdraw Consent – If processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.
